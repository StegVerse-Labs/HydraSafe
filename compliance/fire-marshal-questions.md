# Fire Marshal / AHJ Common Questions (v0.1)

- What gases are stored/used and in what maximum quantities?
- Where are cylinders stored? How are they restrained?
- What detection exists (type, placement, alarm thresholds)?
- What interlocks exist and what do they shut down?
- What is the ventilation/exhaust strategy?
- Where is emergency shutoff and who is trained?
- What is the incident response and reporting process?
- What commissioning evidence will you provide?

Prepare concise answers + references to the permit packet sections.
